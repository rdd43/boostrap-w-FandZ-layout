About

Authored By: Robert Diasio

My name is Robert Diasio, born September 8th 2000. Ever since I was younger I have always enjoyed playing around with technology. My dad taught me how to build computers in our garage, and ever since I have had a love for anything tech. It was not until my sophomore year of high school when I took my first ever computer science class. In this class I found myself addicted to coding. For my final project, I would even upload the files to a flash drive in order to work on the project at home, even though I had ample amount of class time to finish the project, I just kept wanting to add more and more.

Currently I am attending NJIT, have a 3.875 GPA, and pursuing a BS in computer science. My goal is to one day either work for one of the biggest companies in the world such as Google or Facebook, or, rather, open up my own company. Regardless of what path I chose, I know that I want to leave a mark on this world.
